The Crisis

Why Early Childhood Matters

  • 🧠85% of the brain develops by age 6 — the most critical window
  • 🏘️Only 1 in 4 children in rural India access early learning programs
  • 📉Early gaps lead to lasting educational & socio-economic consequences
  • 🌱Early intervention can change a child's life trajectory permanently
Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🌅

Our Vision

Every child thrives in a nurturing early learning environment that builds strong foundations for lifelong success — regardless of where they are born.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower grassroots educators and communities to deliver inclusive, high-quality early childhood education that reaches the most underserved children.

🤝

Our Approach

Training youth as Fellows who work directly with families, caregivers, and children in their own communities to deliver age-appropriate ECE interventions.

What We Do

Age-Appropriate Programs

Our continuum of interventions spans from infancy to early primary school, ensuring every critical developmental stage is addressed.

1–3

Parent Toddler Program

Early stimulation activities and responsive caregiving training for parents and caregivers of infants and toddlers. Building secure attachment and cognitive foundations.

Parent Engagement
3–6

School Readiness Program

Structured play-based learning to develop language, numeracy, social-emotional skills, and fine motor abilities — preparing children for formal schooling.

School Readiness
6–9

FLN / Remedial Program

Targeted support to ensure every child achieves grade-appropriate Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) skills through reading circles, storytelling, and learning games.

FLN Skills
